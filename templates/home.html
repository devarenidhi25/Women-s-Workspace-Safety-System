Frequently Asked Questions

Is my information really anonymous?

Yes, when you choose the anonymous reporting option, we do not collect any personally identifiable information. Our system is designed to protect your identity while still allowing you to share your experience and receive support.

What types of issues can I report?

You can report various issues including but not limited to workplace harassment, discrimination, domestic violence, public harassment, online abuse, and any other situations where you feel your rights or safety have been compromised.

How quickly will I receive a response?

Our AI chatbot provides immediate support and resources. For reports that require human intervention, our team aims to respond within 24 hours. Urgent cases are prioritized for faster response.

Can I change from anonymous to identified reporting later?

Yes, if you initially choose to report anonymously, you can later decide to reveal your identity. We provide a secure process for this transition while maintaining the integrity of your report.

What kind of support can I expect?

Depending on your situation, we can connect you with legal resources, counseling services, support groups, advocacy organizations, and other relevant assistance. Our goal is to provide comprehensive support tailored to your specific needs.
